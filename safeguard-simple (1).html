Safety Tips

Practical guidance for staying safe every day.

Personal Safety

  • Stay aware of your surroundings and trust your instincts.
  • Share your live location with someone you trust.
  • Keep your phone charged and emergency contacts handy.
  • Avoid isolated, poorly lit areas, especially at night.

Travel Safety

  • Verify cab/driver details before boarding.
  • Prefer well-lit, busy stops and stations.
  • Use women-only coaches on trains/metro when available.

Online Safety

  • Use strong passwords and enable two-factor authentication.
  • Keep social profiles private; don't overshare your location.
  • Report and block anyone who makes you uncomfortable.

Emergency Preparedness

  • Save emergency numbers (100, 102, 112, 1091) on speed dial.
  • Add at least 2-3 trusted contacts in this app.
  • Know your nearest police station and hospital.
