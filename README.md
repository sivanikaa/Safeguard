SafeGuard — Women Safety & Emergency Assistance Platform


Because help should be one tap away.



SafeGuard is a web-based safety companion built for women navigating everyday risk — late-night commutes, solo travel, unfamiliar cities, or just the quiet anxiety of walking home alone. It puts emergency help, trusted contacts, and safety resources all in one place, so no one has to fumble through five different apps when seconds matter.

What it does

One-tap SOS — A single, unmissable button that instantly logs an emergency alert and notifies your trusted contacts.

Trusted Contacts — Keep your closest people just a tap away, ready to be alerted the moment you need them.

Emergency Services Directory — Police, ambulance, fire, and women's helplines, all dial-ready, no searching required.

Safety Tips — Practical, everyday guidance for personal, travel, and online safety.

Dark Mode, Profiles & Admin Tools — A polished experience that doesn't feel like a school project, with role-based access for everyday users and administrators alike.

Why I built this

Safety apps shouldn't require a manual. This one was built around a simple idea: in a crisis, every extra tap, every confusing menu, costs time you don't have. SafeGuard strips that away — clean, fast, and built to actually be used, not just looked at.

Built with

HTML5 · CSS3 · JavaScript · LocalStorage — no frameworks, no backend, no friction. Just open it and it works.
